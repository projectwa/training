## 5. üîÅ Reflection + Revision Chain

You can chain evaluation and revision steps for better results:

1. **Step 1:** Generate an initial answer.  
2. **Step 2:** Apply one of the rubrics for self-evaluation.  
3. **Step 3:** Revise the answer based on evaluation feedback.  
4. **Step 4:** (Optional) Re-evaluate the improved version to measure progress.

This process is called **self-reflective prompting** or **feedback looping.**
